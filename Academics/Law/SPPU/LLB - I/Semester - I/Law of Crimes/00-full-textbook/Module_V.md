## ğŸš¨ RAPE (SECTION 375 IPC / 63-73 BNS)

### Historical Context

**Criminal Law Amendment Act 2013** - Landmark changes after Nirbhaya case

### Definition (After 2013 Amendment)

A man commits rape if he:
1. Penetrates penis into vagina/mouth/urethra/anus, OR
2. Inserts any object/body part (not penis) into vagina/urethra/anus, OR
3. Manipulates any body part causing penetration, OR
4. Applies mouth to vagina/anus/urethra

**UNDER circumstances vitiating consent**

---

### 7 Circumstances Vitiating Consent

```
1. AGAINST HER WILL
2. WITHOUT HER CONSENT
3. CONSENT by fear of death/hurt
4. CONSENT believing man is husband
5. CONSENT due to unsound mind/intoxication
6. WOMAN UNDER 18 years
7. UNABLE TO COMMUNICATE consent
```

> ğŸ§  **Key:** Even with apparent "consent," if any circumstance present = RAPE

### PUNISHMENT (SECTION 376 IPC / BNS)

#### Punishment Table

| Circumstance | Punishment | Section |
|-------------|------------|---------|
| **Normal Rape** | 7 years (min) to Life | 376(1) |
| **Custodial Rape** | 10 years (min) to Life | 376(2) |
| **Gang Rape** | 20 years (min) to Life | 376D |
| **Rape causing Death/PVS** | 20 years (min) to Death | 376A |
| **Repeat Offender** | Life OR Death | 376E |

---

### Custodial Rape (Section 376(2))

**Who are in custody/authority:**
- Police officer
- Public servant
- Jail/remand home staff
- Hospital management/staff
- Person in position of trust/authority
- During communal violence
- On pregnant woman
- On woman under 16
- On incapable woman
- Causing grievous bodily harm

**Punishment:** 10 years (minimum) to Life + Fine

---

### Section 376A: Death or Persistent Vegetative State

If rape causes:
- Death of woman, OR
- Persistent Vegetative State (PVS)

**Punishment:** 20 years (minimum) to Life OR Death

---

### Section 376B: Rape by Husband During Separation

Sexual intercourse by husband with wife living separately (judicial separation) without consent

**Punishment:** 2-7 years + fine

---

### Section 376C: By Person in Authority

Person in authority/fiduciary relationship abuses position to induce woman to have sexual intercourse

**Punishment:** 5-10 years + fine

---

### Section 376D: Gang Rape

Woman raped by group of persons

**Punishment:** 20 years (minimum) to Life + Fine

**Note:** Fine paid to victim for medical expenses and rehabilitation

---

### Leading Cases

**ğŸ“Œ Nirbhaya Case (2012) - Delhi Gang Rape**
- Paramedical student tortured by 6 men
- One juvenile (sent to reform facility 3 years)
- One committed suicide in jail
- Rest given death penalty
- Recorded as "Rarest of Rare"

**Principle:** Protection of society and deterring criminal is object of law; courts must impose adequate sentence considering collective conscience

**ğŸ“Œ State of Maharashtra v. Chandraprakash**
- Policeman raped newly married woman twice
- Threatened to burn alive if disclosed
- **Held:** No sympathy for person in uniform; punishment exemplary

---

### Important Amendments

**Act 43 of 1983:**
- Section 114A Evidence Act: Burden of proof on accused
- Section 228A IPC: Prohibition of disclosure of victim's identity

**Act 13 of 2013 (After Nirbhaya):**
- Expanded definition of rape (penetration)
- Enhanced punishments
- Introduced PVS concept
- Custodial rape provisions strengthened
- Trial in camera mandatory (Section 327 CrPC)
- Character assassination of victim prohibited

## ğŸ™… SEXUAL HARASSMENT (SECTION 354A IPC)

### Definition

Man commits sexual harassment if:
1. Physical contact and advances (unwelcome)
2. Demand/request for sexual favors
3. Shows pornography against will
4. Makes sexually colored remarks

**Punishment:** 1-3 years + fine

---

## ğŸ‘— ASSAULT TO DISROBE (SECTION 354B IPC)

### Definition

Assault or criminal force with intent to disrobe or compel woman to be naked

**Punishment:** 3-7 years + fine

---

## ğŸ“¹ VOYEURISM (SECTION 354C IPC)

### Definition

Watching or capturing image of woman in private act where she expects not to be observed

**Ingredients:**
1. Watching or capturing image
2. Woman engaged in private act
3. Where she expects not to be observed
4. Without her consent

**Punishment:**
- First conviction: 1-3 years + fine
- Subsequent: 3-7 years + fine

---

## ğŸ‘€ STALKING (SECTION 354D IPC)

### Definition

Man follows or contacts woman to foster personal interaction despite clear disinterest, OR monitors woman's use of internet/electronic communication

**Ingredients:**
1. Follows/contacts/monitors woman
2. Despite clear indication of disinterest
3. Repeatedly

**Punishment:**
- First conviction: 3 years + fine
- Subsequent: 5 years + fine

---

## ğŸ‘° CRUELTY BY HUSBAND (SECTION 498A IPC / 85-86 BNS)

### Definition

Husband or relative subjects woman to cruelty

**Two Types of Cruelty:**

**1. Wilful Conduct:**
- Likely to drive woman to suicide
- Cause grave injury/danger to life/limb/health

**2. Harassment:**
- For dowry demand
- To coerce woman or her relatives

**Punishment:** 3 years + fine

**Nature:** Cognizable, Non-bailable, Non-compoundable
