## üí∞ THEFT (SECTION 378 IPC / 303 BNS)

### Definition

Taking movable property out of possession of another without consent with **dishonest intention**

### Essential Ingredients

```
1. ‚úÖ DISHONEST intention (wrongful gain/loss)
2. ‚úÖ MOVABLE property
3. ‚úÖ TAKEN out of possession
4. ‚úÖ WITHOUT consent of possessor
5. ‚úÖ MOVING of property (even slight movement)
```

### What is "Dishonest"?

**Section 24 IPC:** Intention of wrongful gain or wrongful loss

### What Property Can Be Stolen?

‚úÖ Movable property
‚ùå Immovable property (but once detached ‚Üí movable ‚Üí can be stolen)
‚ùå Electricity (special law - Electricity Act)
‚ùå Data (intangible - not movable property)

**üìå Pyarelal Bhargava v. State:** Even temporary taking with dishonest intent = theft

---

### Punishment (Section 379)

**Simple Theft:** 3 years OR fine OR both

**Aggravated Theft:**

| Type | Section | Punishment |
|------|---------|------------|
| From dwelling house | 380 | 7 years + fine |
| By clerk/servant | 381 | 7 years + fine |
| After prep for death/hurt | 382 | 10 years + fine |

## üí∏ EXTORTION (SECTION 383 IPC / 308 BNS)

### Definition

Intentionally putting person in fear of injury and thereby dishonestly inducing him to deliver property

### Essential Ingredients

```
1. ‚úÖ Intentional putting in fear
2. ‚úÖ Fear of injury (to person or reputation or property)
3. ‚úÖ Dishonest inducement
4. ‚úÖ To deliver property or valuable security
```

**Punishment:** 3 years OR fine OR both

**If threatening death/grievous hurt (386):** 10 years + fine

## üî´ ROBBERY AND DACOITY

### Robbery (Section 390 IPC / 309 BNS)

**Robbery = Theft + Criminal Force/Fear**

**Essential Ingredients:**
1. ‚úÖ Theft OR extortion
2. ‚úÖ Criminal force used OR fear of instant death/hurt/wrongful restraint
3. ‚úÖ Force/fear used to commit theft OR to retain stolen property OR to aid in escape

**Punishment (Section 392):** 10 years + fine

**If armed with deadly weapon (397):** 7 years to life

---

### Dacoity (Section 391 IPC / 310 BNS)

**Dacoity = Robbery by 5 or more persons**

**Essential Ingredients:**
1. ‚úÖ 5 or more persons
2. ‚úÖ Acting conjointly
3. ‚úÖ Commit or attempt robbery

**Punishment (Section 395):** Life OR 10 years + fine

**If murder committed in dacoity (396):** Death OR Life + fine

## üì¶ CRIMINAL MISAPPROPRIATION (SECTION 403 IPC / 314 BNS)

### Definition

Dishonestly misappropriating or converting movable property of another for own use

### Essential Ingredients

```
1. ‚úÖ Movable property
2. ‚úÖ Property of another
3. ‚úÖ Dishonest misappropriation OR conversion
4. ‚úÖ For own use
```

### Difference from Theft

| Theft | Misappropriation |
|-------|------------------|
| Taking from possession | Property already in possession |
| Trespassory taking | Lawful possession initially |
| Example: Stealing wallet | Example: Finding phone and keeping |

**üìå Ramaswamy Nadar v. State of Madras:** 'Converts to own use' means dealing with property in derogation of owner's rights

**Punishment (Section 403):** 2 years OR fine OR both

---

## ü§ù CRIMINAL BREACH OF TRUST (SECTION 405 IPC / 316 BNS)

### Definition

Dishonest misappropriation/conversion/use of property **entrusted** to accused

### Essential Ingredients (Most Important)

```
1. ‚úÖ ENTRUSTMENT of property
   ‚îî‚îÄ> This is KEY difference from misappropriation
2. ‚úÖ Dishonest misappropriation OR conversion OR disposal
3. ‚úÖ In violation of trust
4. ‚úÖ To another's detriment
```

### Punishment

**Simple CBT (Section 406):** 3 years OR fine OR both

**Aggravated Forms:**

| Type | Section | Punishment |
|------|---------|------------|
| By carrier/warehouse-keeper | 407 | 7 years + fine |
| By clerk/servant | 408 | 7 years + fine |
| By public servant/banker/agent | 409 | 10 years + fine |

**üìå Jaswantrai Manilal Akhaney v. State of Bombay (SC):**
Entrustment is essential - transferor remains owner; transferee gets legal custody only

## üé≠ CHEATING (SECTION 415 IPC / 318 BNS)

### Definition

Whoever by **deception** dishonestly or fraudulently **induces** person to:
- Deliver property, OR
- Consent to retention of property, OR
- Do/omit to do anything causing damage/harm

### Essential Ingredients

```
1. ‚úÖ DECEPTION of person
2. ‚úÖ Dishonest/fraudulent INDUCEMENT
3. ‚úÖ Person induced to:
   ‚îú‚îÄ> Deliver property, OR
   ‚îú‚îÄ> Consent to retention, OR
   ‚îî‚îÄ> Do/omit act causing harm
```

### Punishment

**Simple Cheating (417):** 1 year OR fine OR both

**Aggravated:**
- By personation (416): 3 years
- By false promise of marriage: 7 years (if rape also committed)

## üìù MODULE VI SUMMARY

> ‚úÖ **KEY TAKEAWAYS**
>
> 1. **Theft:** Taking without consent + dishonest intent (3 years)
> 2. **Extortion:** By threat/fear (3 years)
> 3. **Robbery:** Theft + force/fear (10 years)
> 4. **Dacoity:** Robbery by 5+ persons (Life)
> 5. **Misappropriation:** Found property dishonestly used (2 years)
> 6. **CBT:** ENTRUSTMENT + dishonest use (3-10 years based on position)
> 7. **Cheating:** Deception + inducement (1-7 years)
> 8. **CBT by public servant (409):** Highest punishment = 10 years

## üß† MNEMONICS

**Property Offenses by Taking:** "T-E-R-D"
- **T**heft (Take without consent)
- **E**xtortion (By threat)
- **R**obbery (Theft + force)
- **D**acoity (Robbery by 5+)

**Property Offenses by Dishonest Use:** "M-B-C"
- **M**isappropriation (Found property)
- **B**reach of Trust (Entrusted property)
- **C**heating (Deception + inducement)
