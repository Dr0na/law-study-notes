## ğŸ“Œ MODULE IV: OFFENSES AGAINST BODY (2 hours)

### Culpable Homicide (299) vs. Murder (300)

| **Aspect**    | **CH**                        | **Murder**                   |
|---------------|-------------------------------|------------------------------|
| Intention     | Injury LIKELY to cause death  | Injury SUFFICIENT to cause death |
| Knowledge     | Act LIKELY cause death        | Act ALL PROBABILITY cause death |
| **Key**       | PROBABILITY                   | CERTAINTY                    |

**Difference:** Only DEGREE of intention/knowledge

### Murder Exceptions (300) - P-P-P-F-C
1. **P**rovocation (grave & sudden)
2. **P**rivate defense exceeded
3. **P**ublic servant exceeded power
4. **F**ight (sudden, no premeditation)
5. **C**onsent (person 18+)

### Death by Negligence (304A)
**Rash:** Consciousness of risk (higher degree)
**Negligent:** Failure to exercise care (lower degree)
ğŸ“Œ *Jacob Mathew* - Medical negligence guidelines

### Hurt (319) vs. Grievous Hurt (320)

**GH - 8 Types:** E-S-H-M-D-D-F-20
1. Emasculation
2. Sight (permanent loss)
3. Hearing (permanent loss)
4. Member/joint deprivation
5. Destruction of powers
6. Disfigurement (head/face)
7. Fracture/dislocation
8. 20 days severe pain/inability

**Punishment:**
- Hurt (323): 1 year
- GH (325): 7 years

### Dowry Death (304B)
**Ingredients:**
1. Death within 7 years of marriage
2. Burns/bodily injury/not normal
3. Soon before: cruelty/harassment for dowry
4. Burden shifts to accused (113B Evidence Act)

### Wrongful Restraint (339) vs. Confinement (340)

| **Restraint**          | **Confinement**        |
|------------------------|------------------------|
| Particular direction   | All directions         |
| Partial restriction    | Total restriction      |

### Force (349) vs. Criminal Force (350) vs. Assault (351)

**Force:** Motion/change/cessation
**Criminal Force:** Force without consent + to commit offense/cause harm
**Assault:** Gesture causing apprehension

### Kidnapping (359-361) vs. Abduction (362)

| **Kidnapping**             | **Abduction**          |
|----------------------------|------------------------|
| From lawful guardianship   | From any place         |
| Consent immaterial         | Consent material       |
| Minors (M<16, F<18)        | Any person             |
| Substantive offense        | Auxiliary              |
