## ğŸ” Quick Reference

### **Key International Conventions:**
- **Paris Convention (1883):** Industrial property
- **Berne Convention (1886):** Copyright
- **TRIPS (1995):** Comprehensive IP standards
- **Rome Convention (1961):** Neighbouring rights
- **PCT:** International patent applications
- **Madrid System:** International trademark registration
- **Hague Agreement:** Industrial designs registration
- **Lisbon Agreement:** Appellations of origin
- **Budapest Treaty:** Microorganism deposits

### **Key Indian Laws:**
- **Copyright Act, 1957**
- **Patents Act, 1970** (amended 1999, 2002, 2005)
- **Trademarks Act, 1999**
- **Designs Act, 2000**
- **Geographical Indications Act, 1999**
- **Semiconductor Integrated Circuits Layout-Design Act, 2000**
- **Plant Varieties and Farmers' Rights Act, 2001**
- **Biological Diversity Act, 2002**

### **Key Terms (Protection Duration):**
- **Copyright:** Life + 60 years (most works)
- **Patent:** 20 years from filing
- **Trademark:** 10 years, renewable indefinitely
- **Design:** 15 years (10 + 5)
- **GI:** 10 years, renewable indefinitely
- **IC Layout Design:** 10 years
