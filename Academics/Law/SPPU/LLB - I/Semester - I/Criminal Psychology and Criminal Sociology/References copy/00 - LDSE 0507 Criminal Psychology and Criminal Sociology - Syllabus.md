# LDSE 0507: Criminal Psychology and Criminal Sociology

> **SPPU | First Year LL.B. | Semester I**

---

## ðŸ“‹ **Objectives of the Course**

The course is designed to acquaint students with advances made by sociology and psychiatry in understanding human behaviour, particularly **deviant behaviour**. 

The objective of the course is to provide in-depth understanding of:
- Crime causation
- Crime prevention
- Advancement in the science of psychiatry and sociology
- Changed understanding of criminology as a science

At the end of the course, students would be able to understand the causation of crime in a better scientific and rational manner.
