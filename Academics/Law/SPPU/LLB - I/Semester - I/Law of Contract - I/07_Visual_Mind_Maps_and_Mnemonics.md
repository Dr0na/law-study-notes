### 1. SECTION NUMBERS MNEMONICS

#### **Critical Sections (2, 10, 11, 14, 23, 25, 73):**

**"2-10-11-14-23-25-73"**
- **2** = Definitions (a,b,c,d,e,g,h,i)
- **10** = Essentials of valid contract
- **11** = Competent parties
- **14** = Free consent
- **23** = Lawful object
- **25** = No consideration (exceptions)
- **73** = Damages

**Memory Trick:** "Two Ten Eleven Fourteen Twenty-Three Twenty-Five Seventy-Three"
- Think: "2-10-11-14-23-25-73" as phone number pattern

**"A B C D E G H I"**
- (a) Proposal
- (b) Promise
- (c) Promisor/Promisee
- (d) Consideration
- (e) Agreement
- (g) Void Agreement
- (h) Contract
- (i) Voidable Contract
