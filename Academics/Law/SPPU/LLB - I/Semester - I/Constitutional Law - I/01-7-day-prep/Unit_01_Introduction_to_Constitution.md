### Dr. Ambedkar's Three Warnings

1. **Social democracy essential** for political democracy
2. **Contradictions** - political equality vs social inequality must be resolved
3. **Avoid hero worship** and grammar of anarchy

## üìú **PREAMBLE**

### Text (Key Words)

> **"WE, THE PEOPLE...SOVEREIGN SOCIALIST SECULAR DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC...JUSTICE LIBERTY EQUALITY FRATERNITY"**

### Five Descriptors: SSSDR

| Word | Meaning | Added |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Sovereign** | Free from external control | 1950 |
| **Socialist** | Democratic socialism, mixed economy | **1976** |
| **Secular** | No state religion, equal respect | **1976** |
| **Democratic** | Government by people | 1950 |
| **Republic** | Elected head (President) | 1950 |

### Four Objectives: JLEF

1. **JUSTICE** - Social, Economic, Political
2. **LIBERTY** - Thought, Expression, Belief, Faith, Worship
3. **EQUALITY** - Status and Opportunity
4. **FRATERNITY** - Dignity and Unity (Integrity added 1976)

---

## üåü **SALIENT FEATURES** (Top 15)

**Mnemonic: PLUS-DIIF-JESUF**

### Core Features

| Feature | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|
| **1. Lengthiest Written** | 470+ Articles, 12 Schedules (originally 395, 8) |
| **2. Parliamentary System** | From Britain - PM responsible to Lok Sabha |
| **3. Quasi-Federal** | Federal structure + Unitary spirit |
| **4. Fundamental Rights** | Part III (Arts. 12-35), justiciable |
| **5. Directive Principles** | Part IV (Arts. 36-51), non-justiciable |
| **6. Secular State** | No state religion, equal respect to all |
| **7. Independent Judiciary** | Fixed tenure, difficult removal, judicial review |
| **8. Judicial Review** | Court can strike down unconstitutional laws |
| **9. Single Citizenship** | Only Indian citizenship (no state citizenship) |
| **10. Universal Adult Franchise** | All 18+ can vote (earlier 21, changed 1989) |
| **11. Emergency Provisions** | Arts. 352, 356, 360 |
| **12. Special State Provisions** | Arts. 371 onwards |
| **13. Fundamental Duties** | Art. 51A (added 1976) |
| **14. Three-Tier Govt** | Union, State, Local (73rd, 74th Amendments) |
| **15. Amendability** | Art. 368 (different procedures) |

---

### Quasi-Federal Structure

**Federal Features:**
- Written Constitution
- Division of powers (3 lists)
- Independent Judiciary
- Bicameralism
- Rigid Constitution

**Unitary Features:**
- Strong Centre (residuary powers)
- Single Constitution
- Single citizenship
- Emergency provisions
- Integrated judiciary
- Governor appointment by Centre
- All-India Services

**Called:** "Federation with strong unitary bias"

## üìñ **CONSTITUTIONALISM**

**Definition:** Limited government under law

**Elements:**
1. Rule of Law
2. Separation of Powers
3. Judicial Review
4. FR Protection
5. Democratic Accountability

**Distinction:**
- Constitution = Document
- Constitutionalism = Philosophy

**Example:** USSR had Constitution but lacked constitutionalism
