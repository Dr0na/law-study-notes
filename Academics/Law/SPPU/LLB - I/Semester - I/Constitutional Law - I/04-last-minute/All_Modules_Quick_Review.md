### ðŸŽ¯ **Article 14 - Two Concepts**

**Mnemonic: "I-R"**

### ðŸŽ¯ **Article 16 - Public Employment**

### ðŸŽ¯ **Six Freedoms under Art. 19(1)**

### ðŸŽ¯ **Reasonable Restrictions**

### ðŸŽ¯ **Key Cases**

- **Maneka Gandhi** - Procedure must be reasonable, fair, just

### ðŸŽ¯ **Right to Property**

**Original:** Art. 19(1)(f) + Art. 31

## âš¡ **MODULE 09: CONSTITUTIONAL REMEDIES (ART. 32)**

### ðŸŽ¯ **Article 32**

**Right to Constitutional Remedies** - Dr. Ambedkar called it **"Heart and Soul"** of Constitution

**Guaranteed right to approach SC** for enforcement of FRs

**SC can issue:**

### ðŸŽ¯ **Five Writs**

**Mnemonic: "CHAMP"**
1. **C** - Certiorari (quash order of lower court/tribunal)
2. **H** - Habeas Corpus (produce the body - illegal detention)
3. **A** - (Quo) Warranto (question authority to hold public office)
4. **M** - Mandamus (command to do public duty)
5. **P** - Prohibition (prevent lower court from acting beyond jurisdiction)

### ðŸŽ¯ **Article 32 vs Article 226**

| Feature | Art. 32 (SC) | Art. 226 (HC) |
|---------|--------------|---------------|
| **For** | Only FRs | FRs + other rights |
| **Nature** | Fundamental Right itself | Not FR |
| **Territorial Jurisdiction** | All India | Within State |
| **Can be suspended** | Yes (during Emergency) | No |

### ðŸŽ¯ **Public Interest Litigation (PIL)**

- **Liberalised locus standi**
- Any public-spirited person can approach
- For enforcement of rights of poor/disadvantaged

**Leading Cases:**
- Bandhua Mukti Morcha
- M.C. Mehta (Environmental cases)

### ðŸŽ¯ **Nature**

### ðŸŽ¯ **Classification**

### ðŸŽ¯ **FRs vs DPs**

| Aspect | FRs | DPs |
|--------|-----|-----|
| **Nature** | Justiciable | Non-justiciable |
| **Binding** | Binding on State | Not binding |
| **Purpose** | Political democracy | Socio-economic democracy |
| **Remedy** | Court enforcement | Moral/political obligation |

**Relationship:** Complementary, not contradictory

**Art. 31-C:** Some DPs given primacy over Arts. 14 & 19
