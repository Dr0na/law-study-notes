### âŒ **TRAP 2: Number of Freedoms in Art. 19**

**Common Mistake:** Writing "7 freedoms"

**Correct:** **6 freedoms**
- Property (19(1)(f)) **DELETED in 1978**

**Memory:** Show 6 fingers (one missing = property deleted)

### âŒ **TRAP 3: Article 21 Evolution**

**Common Mistake:** Citing Gopalan as current law

**Correct:**
- Gopalan (1950) = **Overruled** by Maneka Gandhi (1978)
- Current law = **Maneka Gandhi**

**Always mention:** "Though Gopalan held X, it was overruled in Maneka Gandhi which held Y."

### âŒ **TRAP 4: Reservation Percentage**

**Common Mistake:** "Reservation can be any percentage"

- **Indra Sawhney reaffirmed 50%**
- Extraordinary circumstances may allow slight breach

### âŒ **TRAP 5: Creamy Layer**

**Common Mistake:** "Creamy layer applies to SC/ST also"

**Correct:** 
- Indra Sawhney: Creamy layer for **OBC only**
- **NOT applicable to SC/ST** (as per Mandal judgment)

### âŒ **TRAP 6: Nature of Article 16(4)**

**Common Mistake:** "State must provide reservations"

### âŒ **TRAP 7: National Awards**

### âŒ **TRAP 12: Article 21A**

### âŒ **TRAP 13: Child Labour Age**

**Common Mistake:** "No child labour at all"

**Correct:**

### âŒ **TRAP 14: Writs - Who Can Issue**

**Common Mistake:** "Only Supreme Court can issue writs"

**Correct:**
- **Art. 32** - Supreme Court
- **Art. 226** - High Courts also

**HC can issue for:** FRs + other legal rights
**SC can issue for:** Only FRs

### âŒ **TRAP 15: Article 22 Safeguards**

  - Persons under preventive detention

### âŒ **TRAP 16: Preventive Detention**

## ðŸŽ¯ **QUESTION PATTERN RECOGNITION**

### Pattern 1: "Discuss with the help of case law"

**Trigger:** Examiner wants **multiple cases**

**Strategy:**
- Minimum 4-5 cases
- With AIR years
- Show evolution if applicable

---

### Pattern 2: "Critically examine"

**Trigger:** Examiner wants **analysis**, not just description

**Strategy:**
- State the principle
- Discuss pros and cons
- Add your reasoned opinion
- Conclude with current relevance

---

### Pattern 3: "Distinguish between"

**Trigger:** Examiner wants **comparison table**

**Strategy:**
- Make a table (minimum 5 points)
- Show clear differences
- Add 1-2 cases for each

---

### Pattern 4: "Constitutional validity"

**Trigger:** Problem question on **Article 14 or 16**

**Strategy:**
- Identify the classification/discrimination
- Apply I-R test (Art. 14) or check against Art. 16 grounds
- Cite relevant case law
- Conclude: Valid or Invalid

---

### Pattern 5: "Evolution" or "Development"

**Trigger:** Show **change over time**

**Strategy:**
- Phase 1 (historical)
- Phase 2 (transformation)
- Phase 3 (current position)
- Use cases to show evolution

**Example:** Gopalan â†’ Maneka Gandhi (for Art. 21)
