## ‚öñÔ∏è **ARTICLE 14**

**Text:** "State shall not deny equality before law or equal protection of laws"

### Two Concepts

1. **Equality before law** (negative) = No one above law
2. **Equal protection** (positive) = Equals treated equally

### I-R Test for Classification

| Test | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **I** - Intelligible Differentia | Real distinction |
| **R** - Rational Nexus | With object |

### Anti-Arbitrariness

> **E.P. Royappa 1974:** Arbitrary = negation of equality

> **Maneka 1978:** Arts. 14, 19, 21 interconnected

**Basic Feature:** Kesavananda 1973

**Background:** 27% for OBC

**5 Key Holdings - Memorize:**

1. ‚úÖ **27% OBC valid** (total 50% with SC/ST)

3. ‚úÖ **50% rule reaffirmed**
