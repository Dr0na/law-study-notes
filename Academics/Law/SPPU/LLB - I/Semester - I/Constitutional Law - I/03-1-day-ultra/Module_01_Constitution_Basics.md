## üìú **PREAMBLE**

### Keywords: **SSSDR + JLEF**
- Sovereign, Socialist, Secular, Democratic, Republic
- Justice, Liberty, Equality, Fraternity

**Legal Status:** Part of Constitution (Kesavananda 1973), not enforceable, aids interpretation
