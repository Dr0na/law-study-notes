### Maneka Gandhi - 5 Principles

1. Arts. 14, 19, 21 **NOT mutually exclusive**
2. Procedure must be **reasonable, fair, just**
3. Law meets requirements of Art. 19
4. Procedure answers Art. 14
5. Court may declare law unconstitutional

**Result:** **Due process introduced** (though word "due" absent)

## üìú **ARTICLE 22: ARREST & DETENTION**

### Four Safeguards - ICLP

1. **I** - Information about grounds
2. **C** - Consult lawyer
3. **L** - Lawyer (right to defend)
4. **P** - Production (24 hours before magistrate)

**NOT for:** Enemy aliens + Preventive detention

---

### Preventive Detention

**= Detention without trial** (prevent future act)

**vs Conviction:** Past act, trial, punishment

---

### PD Laws - NCP

- **N** - NSA (National Security Act, 1980)
- **C** - COFEPOSA (1974)
- **P** - PBMSECA (1980)

---

### Rights of Detenu

**Art. 22(5):**
- Communication of grounds
- Right to make representation

**Art. 22(4):**
- **Advisory Board within 3 months**
- If not, detention illegal
