## ‚öñÔ∏è **ARTICLE 32: RIGHT TO CONSTITUTIONAL REMEDIES**

### The "Soul" of Constitution

> üí¨ **Dr. Ambedkar:** "Very soul and very heart of Constitution"

**Text:** Right to move SC for enforcement of FRs is **guaranteed**

---

### Key Features

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Nature** | **Itself a Fundamental Right** |
| **Scope** | Only FRs (not other rights) |
| **Court** | Supreme Court |
| **Basic Feature** | Yes (Minerva Mills 1980) |
| **Cannot suspend** | Except Emergency (but Arts. 20,21 always available) |

---

## üîê **FIVE WRITS - CHAMP**

### Quick Reference

| Writ | Purpose | When | Against |
|------|---------|------|---------|
| **C** - Certiorari | Quash order | After decision | Lower courts |
| **H** - Habeas Corpus | Produce body | Any time | Public/private |
| **A** - Quo Warranto | Question authority | Any time | Office holder |
| **M** - Mandamus | Command duty | Any time | Public authority |
| **P** - Prohibition | Stop proceedings | Before decision | Lower courts |

---

### Writ Purposes - P-D-S-P-A

- **P** - Person (Habeas = personal liberty)
- **D** - Duty (Mandamus = do duty)
- **S** - Stop (Prohibition = stop)
- **P** - Past (Certiorari = quash past)
- **A** - Authority (Warranto = show authority)

---

### Most Important Writs for Exam

1. **Habeas Corpus** - Personal liberty (most important)
2. **Mandamus** - Command public duty (most common)
3. **Certiorari** - Quash order (frequently used)

## üìä **ARTICLE 32 vs ARTICLE 226**

| Aspect | Art. 32 (SC) | Art. 226 (HC) |
|--------|--------------|---------------|
| **Nature** | FR itself | Power |
| **Scope** | FRs only | FRs + other |
| **Area** | All India | HC jurisdiction |
| **Suspend** | Yes (Emergency) | No |

**Choice:** Petitioner chooses (not both)

## üèõÔ∏è **PUBLIC INTEREST LITIGATION (PIL)**

### Definition

**Litigation for public interest** by any public-spirited person

### Pioneer Case

**S.P. Gupta 1982** (Judges Transfer Case)

**Principle:** **Liberalized locus standi**

---

### Top 5 PIL Cases

| Case | Subject |
|------|---------|
| **Bandhua Mukti Morcha** | Bonded labour |
| **M.C. Mehta** | Environment (multiple) |
| **Vishaka** | Sexual harassment |
| **Sheela Barse** | Women/children custody |
| **Olga Tellis** | Pavement dwellers |

**Exam Favorite:** PIL questions very common!

---

### When PIL Filed

- For poor/disadvantaged
- Public duty not performed
- Victims cannot approach court
- Public interest affected

## üö® **EMERGENCY & FRs**

### Article 359

**During Emergency:** President can suspend right to move court

**After 44th Amendment 1978:** **Arts. 20, 21 CANNOT be suspended**

**A.D.M. Jabalpur 1976:** Infamous Emergency case (right to life suspended - overruled by 44th Amendment)
