## Key Observations

1. **Adultery** is a ground for divorce across all personal laws
2. **Cruelty** is recognized as a ground in all personal laws
3. **Desertion** is available under Hindu, Christian, Parsi, and Special/Foreign laws, but not under Muslim law
4. **Conversion** is a ground under Hindu, Christian, and Parsi laws, but not under Muslim or Special/Foreign laws
5. **Unsound Mind** is recognized across all personal laws
6. **Venereal Disease** is available under all laws except Parsi law
7. **Renunciation of World** is unique to Hindu law
8. **Bigamy** is a ground only under Hindu law
9. **Mutual Consent** is available under Hindu, Christian, Parsi, and Special/Foreign laws, but not under Muslim law
10. **Irretrievable Breakdown of Marriage** is unique to Hindu law
