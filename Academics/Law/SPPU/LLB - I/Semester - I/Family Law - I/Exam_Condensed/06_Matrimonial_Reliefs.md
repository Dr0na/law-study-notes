## üî¥ RESTITUTION [Section 9]

**When:** Withdrawal from society without reasonable excuse

**Effect:**
- Orders resumption of cohabitation
- Cannot be enforced by force

**If Not Obeyed:**
- After 1 year ‚Üí Ground for divorce/judicial separation [Sec 13(1A)(ii)]

**Constitutionality:**
- Upheld by SC in **Saroj Rani (1984)**
- Promotes reconciliation

**Quick Memory:** "Come back order; if ignored 1 year = divorce ground"

---

## üü° JUDICIAL SEPARATION [Section 10]

**Grounds:** Same as divorce [Sec 13(1) & 13(2)]

**Effect:**
- No obligation to cohabit
- Marriage continues
- Cannot remarry

**Duration:**
- Until parties resume cohabitation, OR
- Until divorce

**Conversion:**
- After 1 year ‚Üí Ground for divorce [Sec 13(1A)(i)]

**Quick Memory:** "Time-out from marriage; marriage continues; can't remarry"

---

## üî¥ DIVORCE [Section 13]

### **3 TYPES:**

1. **Contested [13(1) & 13(2)]** - Based on fault
2. **Mutual Consent [13B]** - No fault
3. **Irretrievable Breakdown** - Judicial recognition

---

### **CONTESTED DIVORCE GROUNDS**

**Count: 15 Grounds Total**
- **9** common grounds [13(1)]
- **2** additional [13(1A)]
- **4** wife-only grounds [13(2)]

---

### **9 COMMON GROUNDS [13(1)]:**

**Mnemonic: "AC-DCI-LVR-P"**

1. **A**dultery (voluntary intercourse with other)
2. **C**ruelty (physical/mental)
3. **D**esertion (2 years continuous)
4. **C**onversion (to another religion)
5. **I**nsanity (incurable/2+ years)
6. **L**eprosy (virulent, incurable)
7. **V**enereal disease (communicable)
8. **R**enunciation (entered religious order)
9. **P**resumed dead (7 years not heard)

---

### **2 ADDITIONAL [13(1A)]:**

10. **No resumption** after judicial separation (1 year)
11. **No restitution** after restitution decree (1 year)

---

### **4 WIFE-ONLY [13(2)]:**

**Mnemonic: "BRMR"**

12. **B**igamy (husband's pre-Act marriage)
13. **R**ape/Sodomy/Bestiality (by husband)
14. **M**aintenance decree not complied (1 year)
15. **R**epudiation (married before 15, repudiated 15-18)

---

### **MUTUAL CONSENT [13B]:**

**Requirements: "1+1+1"**
- **1 year** living separately
- **1** joint petition
- **1** mutual agreement

**Process:**
- **First motion** ‚Üí File petition
- **6-18 months** cooling period
- **Second motion** ‚Üí Decree granted

**Withdrawal:** Possible until second motion

**Key Case:** **Vishnu Dutt Sharma (2009)** - No need to prove grounds; consent sufficient

**Quick Memory:** "1 year apart + 6 months wait = mutual divorce"

---

## üí∞ MAINTENANCE [Sections 24-25]

### **2 TYPES:**

**1. Pendente Lite [Sec 24]** - During proceedings
**2. Permanent [Sec 25]** - After decree

---

### **PENDENTE LITE [24]:**

**Covers:**
- Litigation expenses
- Monthly maintenance

**Time:** 60-day disposal target

**Quick Memory:** "Interim support during fight"

---

### **PERMANENT [25]:**

**Forms:**
- Lump sum
- Monthly/periodical
- Secured by property charge

**Factors: "RICO"**
- **R**espondent's income/property
- **I**ncome/property of applicant
- **C**onduct of parties
- **O**ther circumstances

**Modification:** On change of circumstances [25(2)]

**Termination [25(3)]:**
- Remarriage
- Unchastity (wife) - with exception
- Adultery (husband)

**Quick Memory:** "Post-decree financial support; 1/3 income guideline"

---

## üë∂ CUSTODY [Section 26]

**Principle:** **Child's welfare paramount**

**Factors:**
- Age of child
- Parent's character
- Financial capacity
- Child's wishes (if old enough)

**Quick Memory:** "Kid's best interest decides custody"
