## üìù ANSWER STRUCTURE TEMPLATE

**10-MARK ANSWER STRUCTURE:**

1. **Introduction** (50-75 words, 1-2 lines)
   - Define/introduce the topic
   - Mention relevant section/provision

2. **Main Content** (700-800 words)
   - Use headings and sub-headings
   - Explain concepts clearly
   - Include 1-2 case laws (name + principle)
   - Give examples/illustrations
   - Use bullet points for lists

3. **Conclusion** (50-75 words)
   - Summarize key points
   - Modern relevance/significance

4. **Presentation:**
   - Clear handwriting
   - Proper paragraphs
   - Underline important terms
   - Leave margins
