### **Definition**: Bringing Government into hatred/contempt or exciting disaffection

### **Means**:

- Signs
- Visible representation
- Electronic communication

### **3 Mental States Created**:
1. **Hatred** - Strong aversion
2. **Contempt** - Disrespect, ridicule
3. **Disaffection** - Enmity, disloyalty

---

### **3 Explanations - What is NOT Sedition**

**1. Comments on Government Measures**
- Disapproving measures
- To obtain alteration by **lawful means**
- âœ… NOT sedition

**2. Criticism Without Disaffection**
- Expressing disapproval
- Without exciting hatred/contempt/disaffection
- âœ… NOT sedition

**3. Comments on Public Servants/Judiciary**
- On conduct in discharge of duties
- Without exciting hatred/disaffection
- âœ… NOT sedition
