## üü† CRUELTY (Section 85)

### **2 Forms of Cruelty**

**Form A**: Physical/Mental Cruelty
- Willful conduct likely to drive to suicide
- OR cause grave injury (mental or physical)

**Form B**: Harassment for Dowry
- To coerce meeting unlawful demand for property
- OR on account of failure to meet demand

### **Who Can Commit**: Husband or **relative of husband**

### **Punishment**: Up to **3 years** + fine (non-bailable)

### **Punishment**: Up to **2 years** + fine

## üî¥ DOWRY DEATH (Section 80)

### **Punishment**: Minimum **7 years** (up to life)
