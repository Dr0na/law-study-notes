### **1Ô∏è‚É£ NO LIVING SPOUSE [Section 5(i)]**

**Rule:** Monogamy mandatory

**Violation Effect:** VOID [Section 11]

**Criminal:** IPC 494 (7 years) / IPC 495 (10 years with fraud)

**Exception:** Custom permits (extremely rare)

**Key Cases:**
- Sarla Mudgal (1995) - Conversion to remarry = fraud
- Lily Thomas (2000) - Cannot escape first marriage by conversion

**Quick Memory:** "One spouse at a time, or void + crime"

### **2Ô∏è‚É£ MENTAL CAPACITY [Section 5(ii)]**

**3 Sub-conditions:**

**Mnemonic: "CUR"**
- **(a) C**annot give valid consent (unsound mind)
- **(b) U**nfit for marriage & procreation (mental disorder)
- **(c) R**ecurrent insanity

**Violation Effect:** VOIDABLE [Section 12(1)(b)]

**Proof:** Medical evidence essential

**Quick Memory:** "Mind must be sound at marriage time"

### **3Ô∏è‚É£ AGE REQUIREMENT [Section 5(iii)]**

**Ages:**
- **Bridegroom:** 21 years (completed)
- **Bride:** 18 years (completed)

**Violation Effect:** VOIDABLE [Section 12(1)(c)]

**Time to Challenge:** 2 years after attaining majority

**Additional Law:** Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006

**Penal:** 2 years imprisonment + fine up to ‚Çπ1 lakh

**Quick Memory:** "21 for boy, 18 for girl, or voidable within 2 years"

### **4Ô∏è‚É£ NO SAPINDA [Section 5(iv)]**

**Definition [Section 3(f)]:** Lineal ascendant within:
- **3 degrees** through father
- **5 degrees** through mother

**Counting:** Exclude first, count upward

**Examples:**
```
Father's line: Self ‚Üí Father (x) ‚Üí GF(1) ‚Üí GGF(2) ‚Üí GGGF(3)
Mother's line: Self ‚Üí Mother (x) ‚Üí MGF(1)...up to 5
```

**Violation Effect:** VOID [Section 11]

**Exception:** Custom permits (e.g., cross-cousin in South India)

**Quick Memory:** "3 dad, 5 mom - degrees to remember"

### **6Ô∏è‚É£ & 7Ô∏è‚É£ CEREMONIES [Section 7]**

- No specific form mandatory
