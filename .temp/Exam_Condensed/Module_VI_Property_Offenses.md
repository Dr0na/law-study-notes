## ğŸŸ  ROBBERY (Section 309)

### **Formula**: Theft/Extortion + Force/Instant Threat = ROBBERY

### **5 Essentials**:
1. Theft or extortion present
2. Voluntary causing/attempt harm
3. Fear of **INSTANT** death/hurt/restraint
4. Force on **person** (not just property)
5. Force for committing/securing property

### **6 Ways of House Breaking**:
1. Breaking through wall/roof/door
2. Through opening made by breaking
3. Passage by criminal acts
4. Opening NOT intended for entry
5. Opening lock improperly
6. Using force/threat on occupant

### **Example**: Snatching purse with force

### **Punishment**:
- Simple robbery: **10 years**
- With hurt: **LIFE** or 10 years

## ğŸ”‘ Key Distinctions

### **Theft vs Extortion**
- **Consent**: None vs By fear
- **Property**: Movable vs Movable + Immovable
- **Taking**: Accused takes vs Victim delivers

### **Extortion vs Robbery**
- **Threat**: Future harm vs **INSTANT** harm
- **Force**: Optional vs Essential
- **Punishment**: 3 years vs 10 years

### **Robbery vs Dacoity**
- **Persons**: Any number vs **5+ persons**
- **Punishment**: 10 years vs Life/10 years

### **Theft vs CBT**
- **Entrustment**: None vs **Entrusted**
- **Relationship**: None vs Trust relationship
- **Taking**: Without consent vs Lawfully had, then betrayed
