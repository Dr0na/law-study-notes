## üìå What Are Inchoate Crimes?

**Definition**: **Incomplete/Anticipatory** crimes - acts toward committing crime but not completed

**3 Types**: Abetment, Conspiracy, Attempt

**Philosophy**: "Prevention is better than cure"

---

## üî¥ ABETMENT (Sections 45-60)

### **3 Forms** (Remember "ICA")

**1Ô∏è‚É£ Instigation** - Goading, urging, encouraging

**2Ô∏è‚É£ Conspiracy** - Agreement to do illegal act

**3Ô∏è‚É£ Intentional Aid** - Actively helping

---

### **Key Points**:
- Can be by **1 person** (instigation/aid)
- Abettor punished even if offense **not committed**
- Punishment: Generally **same as offense**

### **When Abetment Complete** (4 stages):
1. Instigation/conspiracy/aid given
2. When instigated person does act
3. When consequence ensues
4. Abettor liable from stage 1

## üîµ CONSPIRACY (Section 61)

### **3 Essentials**

1Ô∏è‚É£ **Agreement** between **2+ persons**

2Ô∏è‚É£ To do **illegal act** OR legal act by **illegal means**

3Ô∏è‚É£ **Overt act** generally required

---

### **Exception to Overt Act**:
If offense carries **death/life/RI ‚â•2 years**: Agreement alone punishable

---

### **Key Points**:
- **Minimum 2 persons** essential
- Mental process (meeting of minds)
- Can be inferred from circumstances
- **Dual nature**: Independent offense + form of abetment

### **Punishment**: Same as offense OR **6 months** + fine

---

## üü† ATTEMPT (Section 62)

### **4 Stages of Crime**

```
INTENTION ‚Üí PREPARATION ‚Üí ATTEMPT ‚Üí ACCOMPLISHMENT
(Not punish.) (Not punish.)  (Punishable)  (Fully punish.)
```

---

### **3 Essentials of Attempt**:

1Ô∏è‚É£ Offense punishable with **imprisonment**

2Ô∏è‚É£ Does **overt act** toward commission

3Ô∏è‚É£ **No express provision** for attempt

---

### **Key Points**:
- **Preparation vs Attempt**: Critical distinction
- **Proximity test**: Act must be proximate, not remote
- **Factual impossibility**: NO defense (empty box/pocket)
- **Penultimate act NOT required**: Any act during course sufficient

### **Punishment**: **Half** of main offense

## üß† Memory Aids

```
I - Instigation
C - Conspiracy
A - Intentional Aid
```

### **"AOM"** for Conspiracy
```
A - Agreement (2+ persons)

M - Movement (overt act - generally)
```

### **"IPAA"** for Stages
```
I - Intention (not punishable)
P - Preparation (not punishable)
A - Attempt (punishable)
A - Accomplishment (fully punishable)
```

### **"OOE"** for Section 62 Attempt
```
O - Offense punishable with imprisonment
O - Overt act done
E - Express provision absent
```
