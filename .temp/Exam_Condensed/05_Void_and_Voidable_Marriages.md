## üéØ FUNDAMENTAL DISTINCTION

| **Aspect** | **VOID** | **VOIDABLE** |
|-----------|----------|--------------|
| **Status** | Null from start | Valid until annulled |
| **Decree** | Declaratory | Constitutive |
| **Time Limit** | None | Yes (specific) |
| **After Death** | Can declare | Cannot annul |
| **Who Can** | Anyone | Only parties |

**Quick Memory:** "Void = Never existed; Voidable = Exists until canceled"

## üö´ VOID MARRIAGES [Section 11]

### **3 GROUNDS**

**Mnemonic: "LiSPro"**
1. **Li**ving spouse (bigamy) [5(i)]
2. **S**apinda relationship [5(iv)]
3. **Pro**hibited relationship [5(v) + Schedule I]

---

### **Ground 1: LIVING SPOUSE (Bigamy)**

**Rule:** Second marriage while first subsists = VOID

**Criminal:**
- IPC 494: 7 years
- IPC 495: 10 years (with concealment)

**Key Cases:**
- **Sarla Mudgal (1995)** - Conversion to remarry = fraud
- **Lily Thomas (2000)** - Cannot escape via conversion
- **Priya v. Suresh (2011)** - Burden to prove dissolution

**Quick Memory:** "Two alive = marriage not valid"

---

### **Ground 2: SAPINDA**

**Definition:**
- 3 degrees through father
- 5 degrees through mother

**Exception:** Custom permits (must prove)

**Quick Memory:** "3-5 rule; unless custom saves"

---

### **Ground 3: PROHIBITED RELATIONSHIP**

**Source:** Schedule I

**Examples:**
- Sister, daughter, mother
- Niece, aunt
- [Full Schedule I list]

**Exception:** Custom (rarely)

**Quick Memory:** "Schedule says no-marry list"

---

## ‚öñÔ∏è VOIDABLE MARRIAGES [Section 12]

### **5 GROUNDS**

**Mnemonic: "IMPUF"**
1. **I**mpotence [12(1)(a)]
2. **M**ental incapacity [12(1)(b)]
3. **P**regnancy by other [12(1)(d)]
4. **U**nderage [12(1)(c)]
5. **F**orce/Fraud [12(1)(c)]

---

### **Ground 1: IMPOTENCE [12(1)(a)]**

**Requirements:**
- Non-consummation
- Due to respondent's impotence
- Incurable/unreasonable to consummate

**Time Limit:** 1 year from marriage

**Proof:** Medical examination

**Bars:** Knowledge + consent; cohabitation after knowledge

**Quick Memory:** "Cannot consummate = voidable within 1 year"

---

### **Ground 2: MENTAL INCAPACITY [12(1)(b)]**

**3 Sub-types: "CUR"**
- **(a)** Cannot give valid **C**onsent
- **(b)** **U**nfit for marriage & procreation
- **(c)** **R**ecurrent insanity

**Time Limit:** None specified

**Proof:** Medical evidence essential

**Bars:** Knowledge at marriage; cohabitation after

**Quick Memory:** "Unsound mind = voidable anytime"

---

### **Ground 3: UNDERAGE [12(1)(c)]**

**Age Violation:**
- Groom not 21
- Bride not 18

**Who Can:** Only minor party

**Time Limit:** 2 years after attaining majority

**Also:** Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006

**Quick Memory:** "Minor can annul within 2 years of growing up"

---

### **Ground 4: FORCE/FRAUD [12(1)(c)]**

**Two Types:**

#### **Force:**
- Coercion, threat
- No free consent

#### **Fraud:**
- Nature of ceremony, OR
- Material fact about respondent

**Material Fraud:**
- Previous marriage hidden
- Disease hidden
- Impotence hidden

**Not Material:**
- False wealth claims

**Time Limit:** 1 year after force ceases / fraud discovered

**Bars:** Cohabitation after with free consent

**Quick Memory:** "Forced or fooled = 1 year to annul"

---

### **Ground 5: PREGNANCY BY OTHER [12(1)(d)]**

**Requirements:**
- Wife pregnant at marriage
- By someone other than husband
- Husband unaware

**Who Can:** Only husband

**Time Limit:** 1 year from knowledge

**Bars:** Husband knew; cohabitation after knowledge

**Quick Memory:** "Pregnant by another = husband's 1-year window"

## üö´ BARS TO RELIEF [Section 12(2)]

**3 Main Bars:**

1. **Time Limit Expired**
2. **Approbation:**
   - Knew condition
   - Led other to believe won't seek annulment
   - Unjust to grant
3. **Death of Either Party**

**Quick Memory:** "Time out, approved, or dead = no annulment"

---

## üë∂ SECTION 16: LEGITIMACY OF CHILDREN

**Rule:** Children of void/voidable marriages are LEGITIMATE if:
- Child conceived before decree, AND
- Parties didn't know facts making marriage void, OR
- (For voidable) before decree

**Rights:**
- Maintenance
- Succession to **parents' property only**
- NOT other relatives' property

**Quick Memory:** "Kids legitimate but only inherit from parents"
