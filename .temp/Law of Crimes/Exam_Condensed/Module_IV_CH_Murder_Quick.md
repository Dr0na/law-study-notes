### Homicide
**Homo** (man) + **Caedere** (to kill) = Killing of human being

### Culpable Homicide (Section 100)
Causing death by doing act with:
- **Intention** of causing death, OR
- **Intention** of causing bodily injury likely to cause death, OR
- **Knowledge** that act likely to cause death

### Murder (Section 101)
Culpable Homicide is Murder in 4 specific situations (see below)

## ğŸ“Š Punishment Quick Reference

### Murder (Section 103 BNS)
- Death penalty (rarest of rare), OR
- Life imprisonment + Fine
- Non-bailable, Non-compoundable

### CH Not Amounting to Murder (Section 105 BNS)

**Part I** (With intention or knowledge death will be caused):
- Life imprisonment OR 10 years + Fine

**Part II** (Knowledge likely to cause death):
- Up to 10 years OR Fine OR Both
