## ğŸ”µ MENS REA (Mental Element)

### **Definition**
- **Mens** = Mind
- **Rea** = Guilty
- Mental/Internal element of crime

### **4 Degrees of Mens Rea**

| Degree | Description | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **1. Intention** | Purpose/Aim to cause result | Murder with intent to kill |
| **2. Knowledge** | Awareness of consequences | Selling adulterated food |
| **3. Recklessness** | Conscious risk-taking | Rash driving |
| **4. Negligence** | Failure of reasonable care | Death by negligence |

### **Types**
- **Specific Intent** - Particular intent required (murder)
- **General Intent** - Intent to do act itself (assault)
- **Transferred Malice** - Intent toward A, harm to B
- **Constructive Malice** - Intent to do one act, causes another
