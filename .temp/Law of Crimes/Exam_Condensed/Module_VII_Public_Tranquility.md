### **5 Categories of Unlawful Object**

**(i)** Overawe Government/public servant

**(ii)** Resist execution of law

**(iii)** Commit mischief/trespass/offense

**(iv)** Take possession by force

**(v)** Compel person by force

---

### **Punishment** (Section 190):
- Simple: **6 months** or Rs. 5000 fine
- With deadly weapons: **2 years** + fine

---

## üîµ RIOTING (Section 191)

### **Definition**: Unlawful assembly + Use of **FORCE/VIOLENCE**

### **Formula**:
```
Unlawful Assembly + Force/Violence = RIOTING
```

### **Essential Ingredients**:
1. Members of unlawful assembly
2. Use **force or violence**
3. In prosecution of common object

### **Punishment** (Section 192):
- Imprisonment up to **2 years** + fine + both

### **With Deadly Weapons** (Section 193):
- Imprisonment up to **3 years** + fine

---

## üü† AFFRAY (Section 194)

### **Definition**: Two or more persons **fighting in public place**

### **Essential Ingredients**:
1. **Two or more persons** fight
2. Fight in **public place**
3. Disturbs **public peace**

### **Distinction from Riot**:
- Affray: Just fighting (no common object needed)
- Riot: Unlawful assembly + force

### **Punishment** (Section 195):
- Imprisonment up to **1 month** + fine up to Rs. 1000

---

## üü° PROMOTING ENMITY (Section 196)

### **Definition**: Promoting enmity between groups on grounds of religion, race, etc.

### **Grounds**:
- Religion
- Race
- Place of birth
- Residence
- Language
- Caste
- Community

### **Punishment**: Up to **3 years** + fine + both
