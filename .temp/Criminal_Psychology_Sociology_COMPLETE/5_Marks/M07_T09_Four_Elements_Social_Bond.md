## **HIRSCHI'S FOUR ELEMENTS OF SOCIAL BOND (A-C-I-B)**

---

### **1. ATTACHMENT**
- Emotional bond to others (parents, teachers, friends)
- Sensitivity to their opinions
- Don't want to disappoint them
- **Effect:** Conform to avoid hurting relationships

---

### **2. COMMITMENT**
- Investment in conventional activities (education, career)
- Rational stake in conformity
- Something to lose if commit crime
- **Effect:** Too risky to commit crime - too much at stake

---

### **3. INVOLVEMENT**
- Time and energy spent in conventional activities
- Busy with school, work, hobbies
- "Idle hands are devil's workshop"
- **Effect:** No time or opportunity for crime

---

### **4. BELIEF**
- Acceptance of conventional moral code
- Belief that rules should be obeyed
- Internalized morality
- **Effect:** Conform because it's morally right
